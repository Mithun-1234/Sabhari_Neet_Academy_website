Special Courses

Foundation Course

For Grades 6-10

Our Foundation Course is designed to build a strong academic base for students from grades 6 to 10. This course lays the groundwork for higher learning by introducing students to essential concepts in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, which will be vital for future NEET preparation.

  • Focus on essential concepts in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Evening sessions with regular assessments for effective learning.
  • Preparation for both school exams and competitive exams like NEET.

Integrated Course

For Grades 11 & 12

Our Integrated Course is designed for students in grades 11 and 12, combining NEET preparation with their regular academic syllabus. This comprehensive program ensures that students are thoroughly prepared for both their board exams and the NEET entrance exam.

  • Blends NEET preparation with academic syllabus for Grades 11 & 12.
  • Weekend sessions to balance school studies with test preparation.
  • Rigorous curriculum with regular assessments to track progress.

NEET Repeaters Course

One Year Program

Our NEET Repeaters Course is a comprehensive 1-year program designed for students who have completed grade 12 and are focused on clearing the NEET exam. This course offers in-depth coaching with hostel facilities available to ensure a dedicated study environment.

  • Intensive 1-year coaching program for NEET aspirants.
  • Includes hostel facilities for a focused learning environment.
  • Expert faculty and rigorous curriculum to boost exam readiness.

Crash Course

Intensive Preparation

Our Crash Course is an intensive program designed to prepare students thoroughly for the NEET exam. Starting immediately after the 12th-grade exams, this course runs up to the NEET exam date, focusing on rigorous preparation and mock tests.

  • Focused preparation from post-12th exams until NEET.
  • Includes comprehensive study materials and mock tests.
  • Designed for maximum impact in the final stages of NEET preparation.
