Objectives

When you enter into any new area of science, you almost always find yourself with a baffling new language of technical terms to learn before you can converse with the experts. This is certainly true in astronomy both in terms of terms that refer to the cosmos and terms that describe the tools of the trade, the most prevalent being the telescope.

Our Foundation Course for grades 6 to 10 aims to introduce students to the basics of competitive exams early, enabling them to develop critical thinking skills. The course integrates school subjects with focused NEET preparation, laying a strong foundation for future success.
  • Focus on strengthening core concepts in science and mathematics.
  • Enhances logical reasoning and problem-solving abilities.
  • Introduces competitive exam patterns and strategies.

Eligibility

  • Students studying in grades 6 to 10.
  • Interest in preparing for competitive exams like NEET at an early stage.
  • Commitment to attending weekend classes regularly.
  • Willingness to participate in regular assessments and mock tests.

Course Outcomes

  • Introduction to NEET Foundation

  • Basics of Physics

  • Foundations of Chemistry

  • Introduction to Biology

  • Problem-Solving Techniques in Mathematics

  • Logical Reasoning and Analytical Skills

  • Conceptual Understanding through Experiments

  • Mock Tests and Practice Sessions

  • Time Management for Competitive Exams

  • Course Duration

    6 Months

  • Batch Timings

    Weekends (Sat & Sun)

  • Study Materials

    Provided

  • Mock Tests

    Monthly

  • Available Seats

    25
